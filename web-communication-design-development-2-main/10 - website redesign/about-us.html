Our mission at Outdoor Warehouse Supply is to CONVENIENTLY provide our customers with the HIGHEST QUALITY MATERIALS at COMPETITIVE PRICES while providing the BEST CUSTOMER SERVICE possible.

Our Story

Founded in 2014 by Brad Taylor and Cole Taylor, Outdoor Warehouse Supply was born with the vision of being a one-stop landscape material supplier. With over 40 years experience in the landscape, pool, and outdoor living industry, the Taylor’s decided to take their experience to the wholesale distribution side of the industry.

A strong partnership with The Pavestone® Company, helped kickstart Outdoor Warehouse to a strong initial year of business.

What Makes Us Different?

One thing that makes Outdoor Warehouse Supply different from many competitors is the fact that OWS is both WHOLESALE AND RETAIL. Our vast customer base consists of landscape companies, pool builders, masons, general contractors, custom home builders, and homeowners.

We offer multiple tiers of wholesale pricing based on customer volume and we have measures in place to make sure our contractor’s wholesale prices are protected from the retail customer.

Why Pick Us?

The 2.4 acre Plano, TX distribution facility stocks a variety of bulk soil and granular products, natural stone, Pavestone products, plant material, bagged masonry goods, tools, and other various landscape and construction supplies. We stock over 30,000 sqft. of Pavestone product, over 400 tons of natural stone, and almost 200 species of zone 8A plant material.

Our team is constantly studying industry trends to make any necessary additions or changes to our product lines in order to bring our customers the highest quality materials available.
